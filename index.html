Do You Have Questions?

We have answers (well, most of the times!)

Below you’ll find answers to the most common questions you may have on SIESGSTarena. Also, please feel free to check out our Turning Cup 2021. If you still can’t find the answer you’re looking for, just Contact us!

  arena is a contest platform cum social networking site for people who are interested in and want to take part in competitive programming challenges. People can organize contests and discuss about other contests. People can share and learn about several technical domains.

 Following are the language compilers supported by arena
Language Version
1 C 7.4.0
2 C++ 7.4.0
3 Go 1.13.5
4 Java (OpenJDK) 13.0.1
5 Javascript (Node.js) 12.14.0
6 Python 2.7.17
6 Python 3 3.8.1

  You can visit any contest from Contests page. Open the problem you want to solve and try to come up with a solution which will cover all the tasks (example + hidden). Submit your source code file by selecting appropriate language.

  You can send a request to email given on Contact Page.

  When the contestants take part in arena contest, they may raise or lower their rating that reflects their performance. The information about rating changes and its algorithm will be added soon. An user receives the following titles based on their current rating.
Title Ratings
1 Legend 2000+
2 Master 1800 to 1999
3 Expert 1600 to 1799
4 Advanced 13.0.1
5 Intermediate 1200 to 1399
6 Beginner 1000 to 1199
7 Novice -INF to 999

  Posts are general blog posts written by any members of this platform. These posts can vary in terms of domain and importance. We intend to keep the platform clean and as meaningful as we can by ensuring sensible content is provided to you.
